A fun and learning environment

With over 20 years of combined coaching experience, our FA qualified coaching team deliver football and multi sport activities, developing young people not only in football but in life.

Positively impact a child’s life at every opportunity.

As a football academy for children we offer a high energy, fun, creative and age-appropriate introduction to physical activity through football. It is also a great opportunity for your children to make friends. Our football classes for kids have been carefully designed for both boys and girls, aged 18 months to 10 years. Football academy innovative curriculum emphasises both the physical and social development of our children.
